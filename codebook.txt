Data Dictionary for the tidy_data dataset resulting from the 
Getting and Cleaning Data Course Project


subject - an Integer indicating the number of the subject
activity - character indicating the type of activity the subject was involved in for the measurements
		Possibilities are WALKING, WALKING-UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING
		
The remainder of the variables are all AVERAGES of the designated measure.  		
The first portion of the variable name is the measurement type - for example tBodyAcc 
The second potion of the variable name is the specific measure - mean or std
The third part and last letter is applicable is the axis on which the measure was taken (ie X, Y or Z), not all measures where taken by axis.\
